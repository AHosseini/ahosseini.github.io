Abbas Hosseini

I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student in computer engineering at Sharif University of Technology. I work under supervision of Prof. Rabiee at Digital Media Laboratory (DML) , I am also a research member of AICT Innovation Centre (AICTIC). I received M.Sc. and B.Sc. dgrees from Sharif University of Technology in Artificial Intelligence and Software Engineering, in 2014 and 2012 respectively.

Research Interests

I am Interested in developing scalable machine learning models for statistical analysis of temporal/spatial data to capture the latent dynamics governing the huge amount of various temporal events arising from social networks, online media, financial trading, modern health-care, recommender systems, etc.
My research interests include:
  • Probabilistic Machine Learning: Bayesian Nonparametric Models, Probabilistic Graphical Models, Variational Bayesian Inference, Monte Carlo Techniques
  • Stochastic Processes: Multivariate Point Processes , Survival Analysis
  • Dependent Data Modeling: Modeling Spatio-Temporal Data, Time-Sensitive Recommender Systems,User Behavior Modeling
  • High Dimensional Data Analysis: Sparse Data Modeling, Structured Dictionary Learning, Supervised Dictionary Learning
